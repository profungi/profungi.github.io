Cinema 320 Website Redesign

Introduction

Cinema 320 is an independent movie theater located at central Mass. The business starts from 1982. Over 30 years, Cinema320 devotes to bring high quality international films, documentary films and indie films to the community. It has a group of loyal audience supporting the business. Cinema 320's website is for publishing movie schedules, events, and showing directions to audience.

Research

The research includes notes I took when I did marketing, notes from interviewing audience and the director. I found the current problems and will solve them in the new site.

Problem

  1. Content is too long and the font is hard to see
  2. Direction and parking information is not clear enough
  3. Limited information - no advantage to show business and do marketing

Solution

  1. List requirments from the stakeholders - director, audience
  2. Reorganize the information on the site
  3. User testing and conducting modifications with feedback
  4. Website maintenance training

Content Structure

The structure of the old site is a good reference of the new site. However, there are a few changes:

  1. Add links instead of showing specific content.
  2. The old schedule page is too long. Give each movie a page.
  3. Add an about page.
  4. Add google map to directions and give parking info a header.
